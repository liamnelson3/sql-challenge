titles = Possible Job Titles with an associated ID

departments = lists Departments and their associated ID

dept_emp = Tracks which Department each individual Employee works in

dept_manager = Tracks which employees are the Managers of what Department

salaries = Tracks the Salary of each Employee

employees = Lists all employees and their prelimenary information



X- Each Employee can only have one Salary and vice versa (one to one relationship)

X- Each Employee can only have one title, but the same title is shared across multiple employees (one to many/many to one)

X- Each Employee can only work in one department, but a department can have many employees (one to many/many to one)

X- a department can have multiple employees serve as manager, but an employee can only manage one department (one to many/many to one)
